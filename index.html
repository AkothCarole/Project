About Us

There are over 58,000 children in foster care in Kenya. Children and youth in foster care, like all children, need adults who will love, nurture, protect, and inspire them. They need families who are committed to them unconditionally, so they can grow to their full potential as confident, contributing, creative young women and men. Are you interested in adopting a child from foster care?
